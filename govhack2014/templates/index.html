ARTifacts

This site, created in 48 hours during GovHack 2014, aims to help you discover the artwork that's all around you.

Why?

A list of public art in Canberra is available from the ACT government, but many people don't know that it exists.

By making it accessible via Twitter, we aim to raise awareness of the art in the region.

Who?

Created with love (and caffeine) by the @makehackvoid team in Canberra, Australia
